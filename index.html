Namoona Enterprises Private Limited

Namoona Enterprises Private Limited is an Indian engineering and technology company founded in 2024. The company operates through divisions focused on artificial intelligence, additive manufacturing, aerospace, motorsport, education, energy and consumer products.

History

Namoona Enterprises was incorporated in 2024 with the objective of building indigenous engineering technologies and innovation-driven businesses.

Mission and Vision

Vision: To become a global leader in technology and engineering while contributing to Viksit Bharat 2047.

Mission: To develop innovations in aerospace, mobility, artificial intelligence, advanced manufacturing and sustainable technologies.

Innovations

DIFM-AI and Textual Design Language

Namoona 3D Labs is developing DIFM-AI (formerly CADGPT), an AI platform intended to integrate generative AI with CAD software. The project led to the creation of Textual Design Language (TDL), a text-based representation of CAD modelling operations.

ProtoPrix Educational Toys

Through the ProtoPrix brand, Namoona Toys develops Formula One-inspired educational products including board games, model kits and STEM learning products.

Namoona Aviation

The aerospace division focuses on building hobby grade aircrafts and unmanned aircrafts. It runs the conceptual N38 Trishool advanced aircraft programme.

Namoona Energy

The company has explored VIVACE-based renewable energy systems and future green hydrogen initiatives.

Motorsport Programmes

The Namoona F1 Project is the company's long-term motorsport engineering initiative.
